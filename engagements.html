Dido and Aeneas



Madison Hammel as the Second Woman in Dido and Aeneas

Hammel joins the cast of Henry Purcell’s Dido and Aeneas as the Second Woman, bringing warmth, clarity, and vibrant musicality to this cherished Baroque gem.

As part of the Delaware Valley Opera Company’s 2025–2026 season, Madison’s portrayal brings depth and subtlety to the courtly world surrounding Dido’s tragic fate. Her interpretation is marked by a lyrical soprano tone, elegant phrasing, and a deep sensitivity to ensemble work—hallmarks of her emerging artistry.

This new staging of Dido and Aeneas offers a poignant and contemporary perspective on a timeless tale of love and betrayal, drawn from Virgil’s Aeneid. Under the direction of acclaimed early music specialist John Walthausen, the cast and Baroque chamber ensemble breathe new life into Purcell’s 17th-century score—delivering music that resonates with immediacy and emotional power.

Audiences are invited into an intimate retelling of Purcell’s masterwork, where Madison Hammel’s voice helps shape the emotional heart of Dido’s world.
