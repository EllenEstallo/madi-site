About Madison

© 2025 Madison Hammel

Madison Hammel is a classically trained lyric soprano celebrated for her expressive tone and elegant stage presence. Her repertoire spans Baroque masterpieces to contemporary premieres, bringing vocal versatility and emotional depth to each performance.

When not on stage, Madison serves as a cantor and teaching artist, guiding emerging singers in healthy technique and musical interpretation. She holds a degree in Vocal Performance and appears regularly in concert halls, sacred spaces, and community events.
