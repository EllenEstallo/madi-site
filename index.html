Madison Hammel, Soprano
About Madison
Madison Hammel is a versatile soprano, cantor, and teaching artist, performing across sacred, operatic, and recital stages. With a passion for both performance and education, she brings artistry and clarity to every setting — from liturgical music to opera and voice instruction — connecting deeply with audiences and students alike.
